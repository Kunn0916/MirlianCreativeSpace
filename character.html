My Character

Character Bio

Name: Yuna Celestine

Age: 19

Background: Yuna Celestine grew up in a quiet coastal town where the nights were filled with ocean winds and soft waves. As a child she spent most of her time drawing in sketchbooks and observing the world around her. Because her family moved often, she rarely kept long friendships, which made her imagination become her closest companion.

Over time, Yuna created entire worlds in her mind inspired by the people she met and the places she visited. She now studies art and spends her days sketching in cafés, parks, and quiet corners of the city. She believes every person has a hidden story waiting to be told, and she tries to capture those emotions in her drawings.

Personality: Yuna is gentle, observant, and quietly playful. She tends to speak softly and prefers peaceful environments over loud crowds. She often notices small details others overlook — expressions, moods, and subtle moments that inspire her art.

Although calm and dreamy, she has a slightly mischievous side and enjoys teasing close friends in subtle ways. She values creativity, genuine connections, and moments of quiet reflection.
